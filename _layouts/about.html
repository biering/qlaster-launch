qlaster - your overall social insight

qlaster is a social network where you have better control over your news stream. For better filtering of specific content, you can create or use predefined filters to attach them to your stream. By linking your account to other social networks, you can obtain the content from a wider range. As a result, it is possible to easily organize and adapt your stream to your needs. The presented concept is unique in the area of social networks and will be free for all end users.
